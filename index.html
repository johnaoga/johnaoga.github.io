John's Bio

Specialist in data science and artificial intelligence, I'm currently a researcher at UClouvain (Belgium) in the Mathematical Engineering department INMA/ICTEAM. I teach courses in programming, data science, artificial intelligence, and optimization at Benin's University. I am also a co-founder of MIFY SARL, a company that offers services in artificial intelligence. I'm actively involved in evangelizing AI and Data Science in Africa and developing applications in Africa for Africa.
